A Simplified NVC Approach - Teacher Reference

üîÑ 5-Step Communication Cycle

1Student A Expresses (Fact + Feeling)
"When [specific fact], I feel [emotion] because..."
2Student B Listens (No Arguing)
No defending or contradicting‚Äîjust listen.
3Student B Validates the Feeling
"I understand you feel [emotion] when I [action]. If somebody did that to me, I would feel very bad as well."
4Student A Acknowledges
"Thank you for saying that, you make me feel better."
5Student A Makes Request, Student B Responds
"Next time please [specific action]." ‚Üí "I will..." or "I will not..."

üéØ Key Points for Teachers

  • Act as moderator to help students adjust eye contact, sincere tone, and body language for better communication
  • Stop Student B from arguing about facts immediately - remind them their role is to listen, not defend or contradict
  • Role flexibility: Anyone can start a new cycle if they need to express feelings, and students can reverse roles if both have feelings to share
  • Focus on validation before problem-solving
  • Encourage specific requests rather than general complaints
  • Repeat as needed for full understanding and resolution
This model encourages mutual empathy, active listening, and fair communication
